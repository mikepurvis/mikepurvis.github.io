Detailed Description

The hole structure identifies the size and position of the hole and the offset from the beginning of the packet buffer of the next and previous hole. UINT16_T_MAX == no previous or next. This is safe because the data-block size of 8 bytes means that nothing can start at 0xffff.

This structure MUST NOT exceed 8 bytes.
