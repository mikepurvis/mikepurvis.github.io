Detailed Description

Provide support for the auto-delay feature of the F0 ADC. When this is enabled the ADC will not start converting until the results of the previous conversion have been read out. This is useful if your MCU may be temporarily held up processing something else but is generally fast enough to cope with your A/D requirements. The feature is enabled by default and methods are provided to enable/disable at runtime.
